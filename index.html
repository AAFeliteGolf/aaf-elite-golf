Business Partnership Opportunities

AAF Elite Golf offers a limited number of partnership opportunities for Osceola County businesses interested in supporting the competitive development of an elite junior golfer.

Model: Professional partnership • Not a donation request
Athlete: Ayden A. Febres
Competition: High school, national & international

Sponsorship Opportunities

These partnerships are not influencer-style advertising. They are professional alignments designed to reflect shared values such as discipline, performance, and long-term commitment.

Community Partner — $1,500

This level is designed for businesses that want to support a high-performing local student-athlete and be recognized as part of the AAF Elite Golf community.

Community Partners receive professional recognition through AAF Elite Golf and are associated with Ayden’s competitive program in a clean, respectful way.

Ideal for businesses that value local pride, youth development, and excellence — without advertising or endorsements.

Local pride • Clean association

Performance Partner — $3,500

Performance Partners take a more visible role in supporting Ayden’s competitive golf journey.

This level reflects the preparation, consistency, and discipline required to compete at an elite level and provides elevated recognition within AAF Elite Golf materials connected to Ayden’s program.

Well suited for businesses that identify with performance, growth, and long-term commitment.

Elevated recognition • Performance

County Anchor Partner (Limited) — $7,500–$10,000+

County Anchor Partners represent a small group of Osceola County businesses closely aligned with AAF Elite Golf.

This level may include limited logo placement on apparel or equipment during select competitive events, where permitted and appropriate, along with category exclusivity.

Designed for businesses seeking a premium, high-integrity association with one of the area’s most promising young athletes.

Limited • Category exclusivity

Professional Standards

AAF Elite Golf partnerships are structured to maintain professionalism and protect the integrity of the athlete.

  • Ayden does not engage in scripted endorsements or promotional messaging for businesses.
  • No influencer-style marketing or personal advertising.
  • Select County Anchor partnerships may include limited logo placement on apparel or equipment, where permitted and appropriate.
  • All recognition is handled professionally through AAF Elite Golf.
  • Partnerships emphasize alignment and representation — not advertising.
