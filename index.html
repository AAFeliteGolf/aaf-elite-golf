Sponsorship

AAF Elite Golf offers a limited number of partnership opportunities for Osceola County businesses interested in supporting Ayden’s competitive development through high school, national, and international junior golf. These partnerships are professional alignments — not influencer-style advertising.

Community Partner

$1,500
  • Support a high-performing local student-athlete and be recognized as part of the AAF Elite Golf community.
  • Professional recognition through AAF Elite Golf materials connected to Ayden’s competitive program.
  • Ideal for businesses that value local pride, youth development, and excellence — without advertising or endorsements.

Performance Partner

$3,500
  • Take a more visible role in supporting Ayden’s competitive golf journey.
  • Elevated recognition aligned with the preparation and discipline required to compete at an elite level.
  • Well suited for businesses that identify with long-term growth, consistency, and competitive excellence.

County Anchor Partner (Limited)

$7,500–$10,000+
  • Premium alignment for a small number of Osceola County businesses closely associated with AAF Elite Golf.
  • May include limited logo placement on apparel or equipment during select competitive events, where permitted and appropriate.
  • Includes category exclusivity (limited to one Anchor partner per business category).

Where Partnership Funds Go

  • Tournament travel (flights, hotels, rental car, entry fees)
  • Coaching and player development
  • Training and preparation aligned with elite competition

Partnerships help fund the competitive pathway while maintaining professionalism and integrity.

Professional Standards

  • Ayden does not engage in scripted endorsements or promotional messaging for businesses.
  • No influencer-style marketing or personal advertising.
  • Select County Anchor partnerships may include limited logo placement on apparel or equipment, where permitted and appropriate.
  • All recognition is handled professionally through AAF Elite Golf.
  • Partnerships emphasize alignment and representation — not advertising.

Payment options and next steps are handled directly by email to keep the process simple and professional.
