About Ayden A. Febres

Ayden A. Febres is one of Florida’s most accomplished junior golfers and a rising presence on the national stage. He has been playing golf since the age of three and has earned over 40 competitive victories, distinguishing himself through elite ball-striking, consistency, and composure under pressure.

Competitive Snapshot
  • Florida Junior Tour Championship — Winner (73–68–71)
  • FJT Eagle Ridge Open — Champion (69–69)
  • Round of 16, Junior Amateur Match Play Championship (Boys 13–15)
  • Top 10 finishes in 5 of 6 Florida Junior Tour events (2025)
  • Ranked #3 in Florida | Class of 2029
  • Ranked Top 30 globally | Class of 2029
  • Named to Team FSGA — Class of 2026
Character & Community

Beyond results, Ayden is known for his work ethic, coachability, and professionalism. He approaches the game with humility and focus, understanding that he represents not only himself, but his family, his school, and the Osceola County community he proudly calls home.

High School Excellence

As a freshman at St. Cloud High School, Ayden earned the No. 1 player role immediately and delivered record-setting performances across Osceola County — including a 64 in an 18-hole match (school and county record), a 67 the following week, and a 6-under 30 in a nine-hole match at Kissimmee Bay. He later won individual district and regional championships with rounds of 66 and 65 at Royal St. Cloud Golf Links.

Partnership Levels

AAF Elite Golf intentionally limits partnerships to preserve professionalism, credibility, and meaningful value for participating businesses.

Community Partner — $500

For local businesses that want to support youth excellence and be recognized as foundational supporters of AAF Elite Golf.

  • Permanent recognition on the AAF Elite Golf website as an official Community Partner
  • Seasonal group acknowledgment on AAF Elite Golf social media
  • Permission to reference the business as an official Community Partner of AAF Elite Golf on the business’s website or materials
  • A personal thank-you note from Ayden acknowledging their support

Performance Partner — $1,500

For established local businesses seeking stronger visibility and a closer association with elite athletic preparation and competition.

  • Business name or logo displayed on the AAF Elite Golf website
  • Recognition as an official Performance Partner throughout the competitive season
  • Rotating individual Partner Spotlight on AAF Elite Golf social media
  • Inclusion in select tournament recap or season update posts
  • Performance Partner recognition sign for in-store or office display
  • Quarterly partner updates highlighting competitive progress

County Anchor Partner — $5,000 (Limited Availability)

A premium partnership for a small number of Osceola County businesses seeking meaningful alignment with elite performance, leadership, and community representation.

  • Logo placement on apparel or golf bag during select competitive events, where permitted and appropriate
  • Prominent logo placement on the AAF Elite Golf website
  • Dedicated County Anchor recognition on AAF Elite Golf social media
  • Category exclusivity (limited to one Anchor Partner per business category)
  • Quarterly private updates with detailed competitive progress
  • Personally autographed photo of Ayden as a thank-you recognition piece
